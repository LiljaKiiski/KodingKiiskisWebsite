Bounce - 3 Star Difficulty

The game Bounce involves you controlling a paddle to make sure the ball doesn't fall to it's doom! You have option to change how fast the ball goes, but unfortunately this game can go on forever so to save the ball you would have to play a while...

Created by Lilja (November 2020)

Space Game - 4 Star Difficulty

Boom! Crash! In Space Game you must help the poor Spaceship to avoid getting hit by lazers! You can select difficulty by changing the number of bullets that come per round, and the high score is kept so you can check out your amazing gameplay later!

Created by Lilja (December 2020)

Flight - 4 Star Difficulty

While playing Flight, you control the dog in the airplane. You must dodge the bullets to avoid the airplane exploding and the poor dog dying! And for an extra challenge, you can collect the star coins hidden throughout the game.

Created by Petteri (January 2021)

Galaxy - 5 Star Difficulty

In Galaxy, you create a galaxy full of planets, stars, and more! The objective is to try to make your galaxy live as long as possible, but make sure to prevent it from getting sucked by blackholes, attacked by aliens, or getting stuck in time. Noora Kiiski purchased this game.
