Requirements for each rating

One Star

  • Simple console program, no visuals, and some text input
  • 1 - 10 BottleCaps

Two Stars

  • More Complex console program, a program with no visuals, but some complexity
  • 10 - 20 BottleCaps

Three Stars

  • Simple Graphical game or program, with shapes moving around, Look at bounce
  • 25 - 50 BottleCaps

Four Stars

  • A little more complex Graphical game or program, with images moving around, Look at Space Game
  • 50 - 75 BottleCaps

Five Stars

  • Complex Graphical game, with shapes moving around and multiple levels, Example is Pogo Fish(Incomplete)
  • 100+ BottleCaps
