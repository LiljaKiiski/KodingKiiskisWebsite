Requirements for each rating

One Star

  • Simple console program with no visuals and some text input
  • 1 - 10 BottleCaps

Two Stars

  • More Complex console program, a program with no visuals but some complexity
  • 10 - 20 BottleCaps

Three Stars

  • Simple Graphical game or program with shapes moving around, look at Bounce for example
  • 25 - 50 BottleCaps

Four Stars

  • A little more complex Graphical game or program, with images moving around, look at Space Game or Flight for example
  • 50 - 75 BottleCaps

Five Stars

  • Complex Graphical game, with shapes moving around and multiple levels, look at Pogo Fish(Incomplete) or Galaxy(Incomplete) for example
  • 100+ BottleCaps
