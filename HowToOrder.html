So Want To Order?

  1. Go to the Koding Kiiskis Store and request the program you want made.
  2. Set up a meeting time to negotiate the price, and other details.
  3. At the requested time, we will negotiate the price and estimate your waiting time.
  4. After we have finished your program, you will pay us the negotiated price.

You might also want to look at our Rating System to estimate pricing!
